Research Interests

I research machine learning systems that support human decision-making, with a focus on explainability, visualization, and human-AI interaction. My work explores how interactive visual interfaces, artificial intelligence, and interpretable machine learning methods can improve usability and analytical reasoning in complex domains such as autonomous systems and scientific data analysis.
